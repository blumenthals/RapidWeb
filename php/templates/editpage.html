Basic Editing commands
Headings: ! (Exclamation symbol) Before a word or group of words makes headings. Can combine 2 or 3 for larger headings
Emphasis: '' (2 single quotes) before and after a word or sentence for italics, __ (2 underscores ) before and after a word or sentence for bold They can be combined
References: [ NewPageName] use square brackets around a word for a [page link] to create link to a new page Type full URL or or type a name followed by URL in side of square brackets [URL |http://cool.wiki.int/] to link to a new site
Here are some common symbols you can copy and paste into your text: ® © ¼ ½ ¾ ™ ‰
